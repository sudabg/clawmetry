How it works

Three steps, one command, zero infrastructure.

🔑
Get your key here
One API key identifies your OpenClaw instance. It never expires unless you revoke it.
🔧
Run clawmetry connect
This patches your local OpenClaw config to send telemetry to ingest.clawmetry.com. Takes 10 seconds.
📊
Open app.clawmetry.com
Your live dashboard. Every tool call, token cost, sub-agent, and cron job — visible from any browser, anywhere.

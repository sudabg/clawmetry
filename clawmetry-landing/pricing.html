Frequently asked questions

Is my data safe?
Yes. Cloud Pro uses end-to-end encryption — your data is encrypted on your machine before it leaves, and the server never sees the plaintext. We can't read your agent data even if we wanted to.
Can I self-host?
Absolutely. The self-hosted version is free and open source — forever. pip install clawmetry and you're up in 30 seconds. Cloud Pro is for people who want remote access and fleet monitoring.
What happens if I cancel?
Your cloud data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted. Your local self-hosted dashboard keeps working — you never lose that.
